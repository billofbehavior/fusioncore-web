What it is

An adaptive detection stack.

Sample text — to be replaced. The Storm Center is a research program that fuses several mature open-source eBPF projects into a single, tunable detection plane: protocol capture from Pixie, runtime anomaly detection from Kubescape, and Signal to Noise tuning via the Bill of Behavior reference implementation.

Each component is upstream-maintained; and we work closely with the maintainers.

Components

  • github.com/k8sstormcenter — research org & deliverables
  • Pixie — eBPF observability plane
  • Kubescape — runtime anomaly detection
  • bobctl — Bill-of-Behavior reference verifier
  • ClickHouse — adaptive-write data lake

Research funding

Open by mandate.

The Storm Center is funded by Austrian and EU research programs that require open-source delivery. Code lives in public repos under Apache 2.0 and GPLv2; deliverables are reproducible end-to-end on a local lab.

  • NetIdee — open Internet research grant
  • FFG — Austrian research promotion agency
  • SBA Research / TU Wien — institutional affiliation
