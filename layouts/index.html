What we do

Three pillars, one mission.

The standard

Bill of Behavior — human-friendly, machine-verifyable runtime profiles. The companion to the SBoM, designed for EU CRA and NIS 2 compliance.

→ billofbehavior.com

The research

Kubernetes Storm Center — adaptive eBPF detection stack. Combines mature OSS into a tunable signal-to-noise SOC, funded by NetIdee and FFG.
